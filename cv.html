Short Bio:

I am a Master of Engineering student in Applied Mathematics & Computer Science at CY Tech in Cergy, France. At the same time, I do a Master of Research in Artificial Intelligence & Complexe Systems Modeling in ENSEA , Cergy Paris University and ETIS Lab .

I worked with Digimind Labs GmbH whithin the R&D team for 4 months as a Research Engineer - Intern in their office in Berlin, Germany. My work focused on computer vision and image processing for 3D shape reconstruction. Last summer, I also worked as a Data Scientist - Intern at Karmen in Paris, France, where I worked on optical character recognition using deep learning models, data extraction, and processing.

Currently, I work as a Research Engineer Intern in the R&D department of Median Technologies in Sophia-Antipolis. I actively contribute to advancing generative artificial intelligence solutions in the biomedical field, focusing specifically on lung segmentation and cancer screening. My responsibilities involve utilizing state-of-the-art computer vision and machine learning techniques to drive innovation and improve medical diagnostics.

In addition to my professional experience, I have completed various personal and academic projects over the past years, some of which can be found on the Projects section.
