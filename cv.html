Short Bio:

I am currently pursuing a Master of Engineering in Applied Mathematics & Computer Science at CY Tech in Cergy, France. Additionally, I am enrolled in a Master of Research program focusing on Artificial Intelligence & Complex Systems Modeling at ENSEA, Cergy Paris University, and ETIS Lab (CNRS).

My professional journey includes a 4-month internship with the R&D team at Digimind Labs GmbH in Berlin, Germany, where I focused on Computer Vision and Image Processing for 3D shape reconstruction. I also interned as a Data Scientist & Software Engineer at Karmen in Paris, France, working on Optical Character Recognition for tabular data extraction from financial documents. Recently, I completed my end-of-studies internship as a Research Data Scientist at Median Technologies in Sophia-Antipolis, France. I focused on 3D lung segmentation from CT scans, tumor detection and localization, as well as structural analysis of malignancies.

Apart from my professional endeavors, I have undertaken various personal and academic projects, some of which are showcased on my Projects page.
